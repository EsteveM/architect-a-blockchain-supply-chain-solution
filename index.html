* Harvested stage: This is the first stage in the supply chain. Please, note that to mark a product as harvested you must be a farmer. In addition, you must provide the following data: UPC, farm name, information, latitude, and longitude, and product notes. The farmer ID is assumed to be the currently connected address.

* Processed stage: Please, note that to mark a product as processed, the product must be in the harvested stage, and you must be the farmer who harvested it. In addition, you must provide the product's UPC.

* Packed stage: Please, note that to mark a product as packed, the product must be in the processed stage, and you must be the farmer who processed it. In addition, you must provide the product's UPC.

* For Sale stage: Please, note that to mark a product as for sale, the product must be in the packed stage, and you must be the farmer who packed it. In addition, you must provide the product's UPC, and the price you want to sell the product for.

* Sold (buy) stage: Please, note that to mark a product as sold, you must be a distributor, the product must be in the for sale stage,and the paid amount must be sufficient to cover the price. In addition, you must provide the product's UPC. The distributor ID is assumed to be the currently connected address.

* Shipped stage: Please, note that to mark a product as shipped, the product must be in the sold stage, and you must be the distributor who bought it. In addition, you must provide the product's UPC.

* Received stage: Please, note that to mark a product as received, the product must be in the shipped stage, and you must be a retailer. In addition, you must provide the product's UPC. The retailer ID is assumed to be the currently connected address.

* Purchased stage: Please, note that to mark a product as purchased, the product must be in the received stage, and you must be a consumer. In addition, you must provide the product's UPC. The consumer ID is assumed to be the currently connected address.
